About Us



We Offer Some Of The Best Business Growth Services In Town

Xcelerate is designed to evaluate your enterprise’s growth potential and regenerate the success that has gone stagnant. It’s a tool to assess where you are, ascertain where you want to be and chart a clear trajectory to get there. It maps your enterprise on various performance and potential parameters, both internal and external, and generates a score which compares your potential performance with your peers, your segment and the entire industry. It brings the spotlight back on your business.

Xcelerate is the product of years of experience, hands-on working with clients and deep divining into their issues & suggesting successful remedial actions. It’s the breaking down of challenges enterprises face into recognizable factors, and suggesting feasible solutions to remove them. The program is interactive and involves various levels of engagements to bring out the real bottlenecks, backed by data, feedback and trends.

Xcelerate offers a unique 3-stage program to its clients: Enterprise Potential Assessment (EPA), Enterprise Enhancement Enablement (EEE) and Enterprise Strategy Execution (ESE). These stages involve a unique combination of Online and Onsite Engagements with the client. At the end of the first stage(EPA), each participating enterprise receives a certificate with their Potential Score, which can be a very effective tool to provide confidence to their financiers, employees and other stake holders. Xcelerate should be attended by all enterprise owners, or their strategy heads as the program quantifies potential gaps and reveals critical actionables.

"To create an enabling process for business owners and leaders to evaluate the potential of their business and take action to reach that potential in turn creating growth, innovation and job opportunities."

Enterprise Potential Analysis (EPA)

Features And benefits

EPA is a program designed to assess critical elements of your business. It comes with several features and benefits to deliver the outputs that will enable you to realise your business potential. It offers a scientific and measurable approach to quantify the quality of your business. It works on four major principles- Leadership, Capacity, Customer, and Opportunity to discover your unrealized enterprise potential.

EPA provides deep insight into leadership effectiveness.

It is structured with a unique framework and engagement is both digital & physical.

It uncovers the blockages and brings out your business potential.

It eventually creates a scorecard for the four parameters (Capacity, Customer, Leadership, and Opportunity) of your business and maps it against the industry, segment, and competition, giving a scorecard of your own.

Brings out a neutral view of the potential assessment.

Accountable for removing roadblocks and helps in achieving short or long-term goals.

A healthy scorecard can be a major differentiating factor between you and your competitors and it gives confidence to your employees and all other stakeholders including lenders.

Framework



EPA is a has a logical sequence and hence adopts a stage-wise framework. It focuses on the four independent and cyclical parameters of every business to assess the potential. It takes into consideration Leadership, Capacity, Customer and Opportunity as the four main ingredients. Leadership and Capacity are internal to an enterprise whereas Customers and Opportunity are external. It further breaks the cycle into Competence and Performance related parameters.

    The evaluation process deploys deep study of these four main pillars of your business and evaluates them individually at first and then holistically to arrive at the final score. A lot of time is spent on discussions, interviews and studying these so as to unearth all the aspects which are hindering growth. The collected data is also run through a very intelligent CRM system for errors. The final output is a comprehensive cause-effect analysis with a potential scorecard.
